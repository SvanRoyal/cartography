    Biography

    Sylvia van Royen is a senior at Humboldt State University (HSU) majoring in Environmental Science with an emphasis in ecological restoration (minor in Geospatial Science.) She has been a volunteer intern at the Northcoast Environmental Center in Arcata, California. Currently she is volunteering with Friends of the Arcata Marsh (FOAM) as a volunteer interpretive center staffer. Last year, she was working as both a lifeguard and swim instructor (Red Cross certified) at the Arcata Community Pool. This year she is working at Blondies Food and Drink. Upon completion of her Bachelors of Science she plans to take a some time off to travel. After this academic hiatus she plans to move to Siskiyou County, to gain employment with a federal land management agency. She intends to eventually perform research in the field of ecological restoration, with the hope of improving current restoration practices.
